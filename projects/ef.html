"ef" lyric video

↳ Fall 2025
↳ Design III-A

Tags: Animation

A one-minute experimental lyric video for the song "ef" by chanel beads.

Exhibited in a large scale projection format as a part of the 2025 Undergraduate Annual at the Mason Gross Galleries.

Following the prompt "Recycle, Regenerate, and Renew," I found inspiration from the repetition of the question “will my instinct / be good?” and the natural cycle of self-questioning, uncertainty, and want that the rest of the song describes.
