Hyperlinks...

↳ Fall 2025
↳ Bookbinding, Editorial

A book exercise exploring the cyclical nature of hypertext and the creation of paths, associations, and meanings, however direct or indistinct. Through the physical medium of a book, the experience is non-linear: after choosing a term, the reader is directed to a different page with a related word, continuing endlessly.

6 by 9 inches. Printed on 105 gsm paper and spiral bound.
