Elliot is a graphic designer an artist a cat lover a radio DJ pursuing his BFA in Design at Mason Gross School of the Arts, exploring the web, language, and play through publications and typography.

He is interested in archives, dualisms, websites, information networks, and the everyday. Open to freelance work, he is happy to discuss any projects or collaborations you might have in mind.

When he's not designing, you can find him collecting CDs, going to the library, or making donburi.
Resume & portfolio available upon request.
